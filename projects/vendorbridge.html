VendorBridge

A full procurement ERP — request quotes from vendors, compare them, auto-generate the purchase order, email the invoice, and watch it on a dashboard.

The problem

Small businesses run procurement in WhatsApp and Excel. Nothing connects the quote to the purchase order to the invoice.

What I built

A full procurement system: request quotes from vendors, compare them, auto-generate the purchase order, email the invoice, and watch the whole thing on a dashboard. Built solo in 8 hours at the Odoo × KSV Hackathon 2026.

The hardest decision

Enforcing 4-tier role access (Admin, Manager, Procurement Officer, Vendor) in both Firestore security rules and middleware. Middleware alone only hides the UI; the database still answers to anyone who knows the endpoint.

The outcome

A complete lifecycle in 8 hours, seeded with 12 months of realistic data: 15 vendors, 12 RFQs, 30+ quotations, and 10 POs totalling ₹1.6+ Cr, with auto-numbered POs (PO-2026-XXXX).
